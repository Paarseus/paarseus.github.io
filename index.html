About ARC LAB

Founded by Parsa Ghasemi and Alexander Assal—two Computer Engineering students at Cal Poly Pomona who met on day one. Since then, they’ve pursued a shared mission: build a collaborative space where makers, students, and mentors turn curiosity into capability and spark the next wave of advanced robotics. ARC LAB empowers learners of all backgrounds to design, test, and ship real hardware—safely and openly.

Labs & Research Builds

A peek at what we’re building now. These are living, open projects.

Quadruped Robot (Reinforcement Learning)

Low‑cost four‑leg platform for locomotion research. Train in simulation (Isaac/Unity) → transfer to real hardware with domain randomization.

  • Focus: terrain adaptation, disturbance recovery, energy‑aware gaits
  • Methods: PPO/SAC, curriculum learning, privileged training, sim‑to‑real
  • Status: v0.3 walking; v0.4 adds foot‑force sensing & IMU fusion

Vision & Robotics
